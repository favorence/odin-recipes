Potato Scones

Description

These potato scones, originating from Ireland and Scotland, are an excellent choice of side for breakfast and are a favourite of many families.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cooked potatoes
  • 4 oz self-rising flour
  • 2 oz butter
  • 1/2 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Heat a lightly greased gridle or cast iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Mash potatoes with flour, butter, and salt in a large bowl until a stiff dough forms.
  3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface. Knead dough lightly and roll dough out to a 1/2-inch-thick circle. Cut into 6 equal wedges.
  4. Working in batches, cook scones on the hot griddle until golden brown, 4-5 minutes per side.
