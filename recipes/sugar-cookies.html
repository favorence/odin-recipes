Chewy Sugar Cookies

Description

Soft delectable sugar cookies that are soft on the inside, crispy on the outside.

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups margarine
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup white sugar for decoration

Steps

  1. Stir flour, baking soda, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  2. Beat sugar and margarine together with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth. Beat first egg into mixture. Beat second egg into mixture along with vanilla extract; add flour mixture and stir until dough is just combined.
  3. Wrap dough in plastic wrap and chill for 30 minutes to an hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 350F / 175C
  5. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls; roll in 1/4 cup of sugar. Place cookies 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets and flatten slightly.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned at the edges, about 8-10 minutes. Allow cookies to cool on the cookie sheets for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
